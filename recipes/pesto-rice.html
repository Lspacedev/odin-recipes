Ingredients

  • Onion and butter
  • Rice and water
  • Seasonings
  • Pesto
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Cook the onion in butter, then add the rice and stir to coat.
  2. Add water and seasonings, then bring to a boil.
  3. Cover and simmer until the rice is absorbed. Let stand, covered, for 5 minutes.
  4. Fold in pesto and lemon zest until combined, then serve with lemon wedges.
